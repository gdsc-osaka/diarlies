Account and Data Deletion

The following are the steps to delete your account and associated data for the "Diarlies" app.

This page explains how users can request the deletion of their app account and associated data, in accordance with Google Play Store requirements.

Account Deletion Steps

  1. Open the Diarlies app and log in with the account you wish to delete.
  2. Tap the "Settings" button located at the bottom right of the screen.
  3. From the settings screen, tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. A confirmation dialog will appear. Please review the details and tap "Delete".

Please Note:

Deleting your account using the steps above will permanently remove all your associated data, including your account information (email address, username, etc.) and diary data (including generated text and images), from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

The data deletion process will commence immediately, but it may take up to several days for the data to be completely erased from our systems due to operational reasons.
