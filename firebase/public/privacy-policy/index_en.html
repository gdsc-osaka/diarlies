Privacy Policy

Date of Enactment: May 17, 2025

Date of Last Revision: May 17, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") defines how GDG on Campus Osaka (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our") handles users' personal information in the application "Diarlies" (hereinafter referred to as "this Application") provided by us.

This Policy applies when users use this Application.

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect the following information in providing this Application and use it for the respective purposes:

Information Provided by Users

  • Photo data, video data, audio data, text information (information مصدر for diaries, instructions to LLM, etc.):
    • Purpose of use: To generate diary entries and illustrations using LLM (Google Gemini API).
    • Note: This information is sent to the LLM for diary generation processing but is not directly stored on our backend servers.
  • Location information (including background acquisition every 15 minutes):
    • Purpose of use: To reflect location information in diary generation and to generate diary content based on location history.
  • Exercise data (step counts, etc., obtained from Google Fit or Apple Fitness API):
    • Purpose of use: To reflect activity levels in diary generation.
  • Account information (username, email address, etc., if required for account creation):
    • Purpose of use: For user account management and responding to inquiries.

Information Generated by LLM

  • Diary entries and illustration data:
    • Purpose of use: For users to view and manage diaries within this Application, and to make them public to other users through the social function (if the user chooses public settings).
    • Note: This generated data is stored on our servers.

Information Automatically Acquired Through Use of the Application

  • Application usage information (using Firebase Analytics):
    • Examples: features used, frequency of use, crash reports, etc.
    • Purpose of use: To improve the service of this Application, analyze usage, and fix bugs.
  • Device information (using Firebase Crashlytics):
    • Examples: OS type/version, device model, device ID (processed into a non-personally identifiable format).
    • Purpose of use: To investigate the cause of crashes and improve the service of this Application.

Users can refuse to provide some information by adjusting the settings within this Application or their OS. However, in such cases, some features of this Application may not be available.

3. Provision of Information to Third Parties

We will not provide users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • Provision to LLM (Google Gemini API):
    • For the purpose of generating diary entries and illustrations, information provided by users (photos, videos, audio, text information, location data, exercise data, etc.) will be sent to the Gemini API provided by Google LLC.
    • Regarding the handling of information by the Gemini API, please check the privacy policy and terms of use of Google LLC.
  • Disclosure to other users (Social Function):
    • If a user sets their account and diary to "public," the generated diary entries and illustrations will be viewable by other users of this Application. Users can change their public settings at any time.
  • Provision to information collection module providers:
    • In connection with the use of Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, application usage information and device information will be provided to Google LLC in a non-personally identifiable form.
  • With the user's consent.
  • When required by law.
  • When it is necessary for the protection of a person's life, body, or property, and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual.
  • When it is particularly necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound upbringing of children, and it is difficult to obtain the consent of the individual.
  • When it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, a local government, or an individual or entity entrusted by either of the foregoing in executing affairs prescribed by law, and obtaining the consent of the individual is likely to impede the execution of the affairs.

4. Management and Protection of Information

We will take appropriate security measures to prevent the loss, theft, leakage, alteration, and unauthorized access of information collected from users.

When sending user data to the LLM and during communication with the server, we use HTTPS encrypted communication.

Appropriate measures will also be taken to protect generated diary data stored on the server from unauthorized access.

5. User Rights

Disclosure, Correction, Addition, Deletion, Suspension of Use, etc., of Information:

Users may request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, or erasure (hereinafter referred to as "Disclosure, etc.") of their personal information held by us.

For operations that users can perform themselves within this Application (e.g., deleting diaries, editing/deleting account information), please use the features within the app. If an account is deleted, all related diary data will also be deleted.

For requests for Disclosure, etc., other than those mentioned above, please contact us at the "8. Contact Information" below. After confirming your identity, we will respond promptly within a reasonable scope.

Withdrawal of Consent:

Users may withdraw their consent to the use of personal information, in whole or in part, at any time. If consent is withdrawn, some features of this Application may not be available.

6. Use by Minors

This Application is intended for users aged 16 and over.

If a person under the age of 16 wishes to use this Application, they must do so with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. If we discover that a user under the age of 18 is using this Application without the consent of a legal guardian, we may delete the user's account and related information.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may revise this Policy due to changes in laws or the service content of this Application.

If we change this Policy, we will notify users of the content of the revised policy and its effective date by in-app notification or other methods deemed appropriate by us.

The revised Privacy Policy will become effective from the effective date notified (generally one month after notification, but this may be adjusted depending on the nature of the changes).
